About

BFP Collective

BFP Collective (Bernardi Fitness & Performance LLC) is a structured coaching system for basketball players, athletes, and individuals who want clear direction, progression, and accountability.

Built by Brian Bernardi: 5 years of Division 1 basketball experience; Southern Methodist University under Hall of Fame Coach Larry Brown, former NBA point guard Eric Snow, former NBA player George Lynch; Hofstra University under Coach Joe Mihalich, NBA Champion Coach Speedy Claxton, and current University of Notre Dame assistant coach Mike Farrelly.

1,300+ career college points; Top 5 all-time 3PT shooter at Hofstra University (in only 3 years); professional basketball experience in the top leagues of the Czech Republic and Georgia; amateur basketball experience in Germany, Netherlands, and Belgium under University of San Francisco Head Coach Jay Duncan.

HYROX Pro Individual time: 1:14:46. Coaching experience with youth/teens, high school, college, and adult athletes.

What It Takes

I Want To Make A Real Difference And Help You Reach Your Goals

Stay on track or adjust, but keep moving forward.

01

Structured Progressive Planning

Where is your current level, and what is needed to begin improving? If you do too little or keep doing the same thing, you do not make progress. But if you do too much too soon, you risk injury, burnout, and setbacks. Training should be structured, progressive, and adjusted over time. We’ll help figure out what works best for you and your goals.

02

Discipline & Consistency

1–2 random workout sessions per week will not get you to where you want to be anytime soon. Let’s fully understand your goals, your “why,” and build a plan that works with your schedule and lifestyle. Real progress comes from consistency over time.

03

Community, Communication & Accountability

Be around like-minded people with similar goals who push each other to improve. BFP Collective is more than workouts. It’s a team, a community, and an environment built around growth, communication, and accountability. We’ll help keep you on track and doing what’s needed to continue improving.

04

Belief

Believe that you can do great things and don’t be afraid to fail big. Too many people never fully pursue their goals, waste their talent, or hold themselves back before they even start. Let’s change that.

Policy, Session Lengths & Contact

Booking, Membership Notes & Location

A limited number of athletes will be part of the BFP system as it continues to grow with full training facilities, with priority scheduling within the system, eligibility for grandfathered pricing as programs expand, and first access to future training opportunities.

Session Lengths

1-on-1: 60 minutes. Group: 60 minutes. Team: 90–120 minutes. Adult Training: 60 minutes.

Booking & Policy

Sessions cancelled with at least 24-hour notice will receive a session credit for future use. Late cancellations (within 24 hours) and no-shows will result in the session being used. If BFP Collective cancels due to weather, emergency, or scheduling conflict, the session will be credited or rescheduled.

Guests & Group Sessions

Elite Members may bring 1 guest to group sessions. Non-members attending group sessions are $35 per athlete.
