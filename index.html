What It Takes

More Than Random Workouts. A System Built For Progress.

Real improvement requires structure, consistency, communication, and the belief that you can pursue big goals without holding yourself back.

Structured Progressive Planning

Training Built Around Your Level

Where are you right now, and what is needed to begin improving? Too little and you do not make progress. Too much too soon and you risk injury, burnout, and setbacks.

Training should be structured, progressive, and adjusted over time. We help figure out what works best for you and your goals.

Discipline & Consistency

More Than 1–2 Random Sessions

One or two random workouts per week will not get you to where you want to be anytime soon.

We work to fully understand your goals and your “why,” and build a plan that fits your schedule and lifestyle. Real progress comes from consistency over time.

Community, Communication & Accountability

Environment Matters As Much As Programming

Be around like-minded people with similar goals who push each other to improve.

BFP Collective is more than workouts. It is a team, a community, and an environment built around growth, communication, and accountability. We help keep you on track.

Belief

Believe You Can Do Great Things

Too many people never fully pursue their goals, waste their talent, or hold themselves back before they even start.

Believe that you can do great things and do not be afraid to fail big. Let’s change the pattern and move toward what you are capable of.

Success, Failure, or Setback? #WhatsNext — stay on track or adjust, but keep moving forward.

About

BFP Collective · Built From Real Playing & Coaching Experience

BFP Collective (Bernardi Fitness & Performance LLC) is a structured coaching system for basketball players, athletes, and individuals who want clear direction, progression, and accountability.

Built by Brian Bernardi, a former Division 1 and professional player, HYROX Pro Individual athlete, and experienced coach for youth, high school, college, and adult athletes.

  • 5 years of Division 1 basketball experience.
  • Southern Methodist University under Hall of Fame Coach Larry Brown, former NBA point guard Eric Snow, and former NBA player George Lynch.
  • Hofstra University under Coach Joe Mihalich, NBA Champion Coach Speedy Claxton, and current University of Notre Dame assistant coach Mike Farrelly.
  • 1,300+ career college points.
  • Top 5 all-time 3PT shooter at Hofstra University (in only 3 years).
  • Professional basketball experience in the top leagues of the Czech Republic and Georgia.
  • Amateur basketball experience in Germany, Netherlands, and Belgium under University of San Francisco Head Coach Jay Duncan.
  • HYROX Pro Individual time: 1:14:46.
  • Coaching experience with youth/teens, high school, college, and adult athletes.
